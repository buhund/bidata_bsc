# Test Feedback

### 1. Change overview to display June
- The client finds the monthly toggle bar with ease.

### 2. Find the Budget page
- The client clicks the Budget button on the left hand side bar.

### 3. Add a new (single) income to the budget
- The Add income button is pressed and the entry is added without any difficulty.

### 4. Set a recurring income
TASK: You work weekend shifts at Bunnpris. You make about 8960 kr a month. Add this as a recurring income.
- This task is done without any problems or hick ups.

### 5. Add a new expense to the budget
- The client has no problem preforming this task.

### 6. Find the accounting page
- The mouse pointer goes directly to the left side bar and clicks the "Accounting" button.

### 7. Add a new income to the accounting
TASK: You need to pay your electricity bill, but have spent too much on beer. Therefore you had to sell your stamp collection on Finn. You sold it for 4600 kr. Add this income to the budget for January.
- This is done without any difficulty.

### 8. Add new expense to the accounting
TASK: You were social this weekend. You ate at Kompis for 340 kr. Afterwards you went to the cinema, which cost 170 kr. Add this
- This is also done without any problems.

### 9. Find the expense from 12.03.2023
- The client does this without any problems.

### 10. Find your scanned receipts
TASK: Your camera mysteriously died just as you were about to photograph a real Yeti! Now you  must get it repaired, and have to find your receipt for it. You remember buying it in March, but not the exact date. Find the receipt.
- The client navigates to the "Accounting" page, find the month selector and clicks on March. 

### 11. Determine whether you made a profit or a loss from the previous month
- The client goes to the "Dashboard" page and looks at the Overview. The client sees the summarization bar at the bottom of the page but is rather confused by the numbers that are displayed. 

### 12. Add a new category
TASK: You want to start collecting stamps again, and need to have that as it's own category in your budget and accounting. Add the new category "Stamps".
- The "Settings" button on the left side par is clicked. Afterwards the client find the "Categories" tap and adds a new category.

### 13. Edit an existing category
TASK: You noticed that you had misspelled "Miscelleanous", i.e. you wrote "Muskelenius". Update the category name and correct the misspelling.
- The category is selected and then the client clicks the "Edit" button and changes the name without problem.

### 14. General feedback
- The client wished for monthly trend in the left chart on the Dashboard page. He also expressed wishes to add an "+" sign in the "add entry" function on the accounting page. The client liked having red and green color representation in the program's charts. Finally, client found the summarization bar in the "Dashboard" page less intuitive then hoped for.
